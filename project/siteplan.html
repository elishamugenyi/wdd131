Site Name: StudentPay.com

Site Purpose:

There is considerably many students who want to access higher education outside Africa. However,these students do not have means of making the international transactions through their banks, because the banks do not support the platforms. This site will make it easy for students to make their payments to their universities.

Scenarios:

I do not have a credit card, how can i pay for my school?

I want to study in an international university, but how do i make a payment to the University?

Color Schema:

Secondary color will be Black.

Auxilliary color will be lime Green.
